## üåê Domain Options

Your domain name (yourcompany.com) is your online address. Here are your options:

### We Manage Your Domain

- We register and manage the domain
- Handle all technical configuration
- Manage renewals automatically
- SSL certificate included

**Pros:** Simplest option, nothing for you to manage
**Cons:** Domain registered in our name (transferable anytime)

### You Own, We Configure

- You purchase the domain from any registrar
- We configure DNS settings for your website
- You maintain ownership and billing
- SSL certificate included

**Pros:** Full ownership from day one
**Cons:** You manage renewals and billing

### Full Self-Management

- You purchase and manage the domain
- We provide DNS configuration instructions
- You handle all domain settings
- We assist with initial setup

**Pros:** Complete control
**Cons:** Requires some technical knowledge
