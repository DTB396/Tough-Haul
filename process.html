How We Work

Our Development Process

We believe great websites come from great communication. Our process is designed to keep you informed, involved, and confident at every stage. No surprises, no confusion—just a clear path from idea to launch.

1

Discovery & Strategy

We start by understanding your business, your customers, and your goals. This isn't just about what you want your website to look like—it's about what you want it to accomplish.

What happens:

  • Free consultation call (30-45 minutes)
  • Review of your current online presence
  • Discussion of your target customers
  • Identification of key website goals
  • Competitor and industry research

What you get:

  • Clear understanding of project scope
  • Initial recommendations
  • Timeline and budget expectations
2

Proposal & Agreement

You receive a detailed proposal with everything spelled out: scope, timeline, pricing, and deliverables. No hidden fees, no vague promises—everything in writing.

What happens:

  • Detailed proposal delivered (usually within 48 hours)
  • Scope of work clearly defined
  • Timeline with milestones
  • Transparent pricing breakdown
  • Questions answered before commitment

What you get:

  • Written proposal you can review
  • No-pressure decision making
  • Clear agreement terms
3

Content Gathering

We help you organize the content for your website. Don't have everything ready? No problem—we guide you through what's needed and can help with writing.

What we need from you:

  • Logo and brand colors
  • Service/product descriptions
  • Photos of your work
  • Customer testimonials
  • Company information

What we provide:

  • Content checklist and templates
  • Writing assistance (if included)
  • Photo optimization
  • Content organization
4

Design & Development

This is where your website comes to life. We design and build with regular check-ins so you see progress and can provide feedback throughout.

What happens:

  • Homepage design mockup for approval
  • Development of all pages
  • Mobile responsiveness testing
  • SEO optimization implementation
  • Regular progress updates

Your involvement:

  • Review design concepts
  • Provide feedback (usually 2-3 revision rounds)
  • Approve final design before launch
5

Review & Testing

Before launch, we thoroughly test everything and give you a private preview to review and request any final changes.

What we test:

  • All links and forms working correctly
  • Mobile and tablet display
  • Browser compatibility
  • Loading speed optimization
  • SEO checklist verification

What you do:

  • Review preview link
  • Check all content for accuracy
  • Request final changes
  • Give launch approval
6

Launch & Support

Go live with confidence! We handle the technical deployment and provide training and support to ensure your continued success.

What happens:

  • Domain configuration (if needed)
  • Website deployment to live server
  • SSL certificate activation
  • Google Analytics verification
  • Search console submission

What you get:

  • Training on your management option
  • Documentation for reference
  • 30-day post-launch support
  • Ongoing management (if selected)

Typical Timeline
