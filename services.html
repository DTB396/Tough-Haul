Our Services

Tillerstead LLC provides standards-based tile installation, waterproofing, and bathroom remodeling for South Jersey homeowners. Every project is built to TCNA 2024 and New Jersey HIC standards—no shortcuts, no mystery crews.

Tile Installation

Floor, wall, and specialty tile installed with careful prep, movement accommodation, and manufacturer-approved mortars. We follow TCNA and ANSI standards for every assembly.

Waterproofing Systems

Wet-area waterproofing (showers, tub surrounds, floors) built as complete assemblies—membranes, transitions, and drains—then verified before tile. We use ANSI A118.10/A118.12 products and document key phases for your records.

Bathroom Remodeling

Full and partial bathroom remodels, including demolition, substrate repair, tile, waterproofing, and finish carpentry. All work is owner-supervised and fully permitted as required.

Code & Compliance

Licensed and insured (NJ HIC #13VH10808800). All work meets or exceeds TCNA 2024, ANSI, and New Jersey code requirements. Documentation and warranty provided.
