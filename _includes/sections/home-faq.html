How do you determine pricing?

We price by how much space your items take in our truck, not by the hour. We'll give you an upfront quote before we start—the price we quote is the price you pay. No hidden fees!

Do you offer same-day service?

Yes! We offer same-day junk removal for most jobs. Give us a call in the morning and we can often be there the same afternoon.

What items can you haul?

We take almost everything—furniture, appliances, electronics, mattresses, yard debris, construction waste, and more. The only things we can't take are hazardous materials, chemicals, and certain regulated items.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes! Tough Haul is fully licensed, bonded, and insured for your protection and peace of mind.
