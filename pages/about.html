About Tillerstead LLC

Tillerstead LLC delivers standards-based tile installations with written scope, verified execution, and photo documentation. Licensed New Jersey HIC #13VH10808800, serving Atlantic, Ocean & Cape May Counties. Projects prioritize substrate verification, wet-area waterproofing per the selected system, and records that support future maintenance and resale.

Why Details Matter: Standards, Not Guesswork

Tile failures usually start underneath the tile: prep, flatness, moisture management, and movement accommodation. We verify substrate conditions with appropriate straightedges/levels for the space and tile format, then build the assembly to the requirements of the selected method and materials.

Mortars and membranes aren’t “one-size-fits-all.” Material selection follows published specifications (ANSI A118 classifications, manufacturer data sheets, and the requirements of the installation method). Movement accommodation is planned and installed per TCNA EJ171 where required.

This approach takes more effort than “some guy,” but it reduces the risk of cracked tile, leaks, and avoidable remediation. When wet areas are involved, waterproofing is installed per the system requirements and flood tested where required by the TCNA method or manufacturer—then documented.
