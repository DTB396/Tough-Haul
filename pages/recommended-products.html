FAQ

Do affiliate links increase my cost?
No. Amazon’s pricing is the same. The commission is paid by Amazon.
Are these the exact products you use?
Often, yes—especially tools. For materials (mortar/membrane/grout), we sometimes source locally for speed and freshness. If you need an exact match, confirm the manufacturer data sheet and batch availability.
Do these products guarantee a “code-compliant” installation?
No product guarantees a compliant install. Compliance comes from using the right product for the application and installing it per manufacturer instructions and recognized methods (e.g., TCNA) with proper substrate prep and detailing.
What if a product is out of stock or a link breaks?
Amazon listings change. Search by the product name/brand. If you notice a broken link, tell us and we’ll update it.
