Tile Installation & Waterproofing

Tile isn’t just adhesion—it’s an assembly. We build tile systems designed to handle moisture, movement, and real-world use. Work follows TCNA 2024 methods and applicable ANSI A108/A118 standards, with movement accommodation planned per TCNA EJ171 where required.

  • Wet-area waterproofing systems: Bonded waterproofing membranes meeting ANSI A118.10 (sheet or liquid-applied) installed per the selected system requirements, with seams and penetrations detailed accordingly. Flood testing performed where required by the TCNA method or manufacturer specification.
  • Large-format tile: Substrate flatness is verified to the tolerances required for the tile format. Mortar/trowel selection and setting techniques are chosen to meet TCNA/ANSI coverage requirements, with lippage-control methods used where appropriate.
  • Backsplashes: Clean outlet cutouts, straight lines, and finished terminations. Transitions at changes of plane are detailed with appropriate sealants for the application.
  • Floor tile: Underlayment/uncoupling strategies selected based on substrate and conditions (per product requirements). Movement joints planned at perimeters and changes of plane per EJ171 where required.
  • Heated floors: Electric radiant systems installed per manufacturer requirements, with documented resistance testing stages where applicable, and controls/GFCI protections coordinated per the chosen system.
  • Niches & details: Prefab or site-built niches and benches detailed to maintain waterproofing continuity and positive drainage where required.
